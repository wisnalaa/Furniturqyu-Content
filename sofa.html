Choosing the Right Material

Fabric

Fabric is a versatile choice for sofas due to its wide variety of colors, patterns, and textures. It is easy to maintain and can often be cleaned or laundered, making it suitable for homes with children or pets.

Leather

Leather is a timeless and luxurious option known for its durability and rich appearance. Available in types such as full-grain, top-grain, and bonded leather, each offering unique textures and qualities.

Microfiber

Microfiber is a synthetic fabric known for its softness, durability, and stain resistance. Offers a comfortable and affordable alternative to leather with a similar luxurious feel.
